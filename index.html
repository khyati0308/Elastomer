Products

We offer our clients a wide range of after market rubberized products and auto rubber parts like silicone rubber parts, automobile rubber parts, industrial rubber parts, metal bonded rubber parts, automotive rubber products, nitrile rubber products, poly rubber products, metal bonded rubber products, bonded rubber mounting, silicone rubber mounting, thick rubber mounting, anti vibration rubber mounting, etc. Our range finds application in various industrial and automotive requirements. We use fine grade raw material like Rubber, Fluorocarbon Rubber (FPM), Nitrile Rubber (NBR, Acrylonitrile-Butadiene Rubber), Poly Rubber (ACM, Acrylic Acid Ester Rubber) and Silicone Rubber (VMQ) in fabricating our assortment. We supply our automotive rubber parts to some prominent automobile companies like Suzuki, Toyota, Nissan, Isuzu, Mercedes and Volvo through our dealers spread across the globe.

Silicone rubber is an elastomer (rubber-like material) composed of silicone—itself a polymer—containing silicon together with carbon, hydrogen, and oxygen. Silicone rubbers are widely used in industry, and there are multiple formulations.
